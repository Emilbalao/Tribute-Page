Here's a time line of Kevin Durant's life:

  • 1988 (Kevin's Birth) - Kevin Durant was born on September 29, 1988. He was raised by his single mother with his older brother. Ever since he was ten years old he wanted to be an NBA player.
  • 1998 (Kevin's father leaves) - Kevin Durants father leaves before Kevin can get to know him. Kevin now has to be raised by his single mother.
  • 1997 (The Durant's dominate sports) - Kevin Durant and his brother loved to play sports when they were young. They played many different sports. When a freshman Kevin's brother, Tony, transfered to a military school. Kevin became the onlt star athlete with the name Durant a his school.
  • 1999 (Mom's help) - Kevin Durant's mother helped him become what he is today. His mother helped him train and made him do sprints. They have paid off.
  • 2001 (Durant's dad comes back) - Kevin Durants father comes back into his life. Kevin is 13 years old and has been raised by his mother all 13 of these years.
  • 2005 (High School) - Kevin Duant played his high school basketball at Montrose Christian School. He is 6'9" ans started for his team. He became a MCDonald's All-American and won Co-MVP award.
  • 2006 (College) - Kevin Durant played his college basketball at the University of Texas. He started as a freshman and that would be the only season that he would play at Texas.
  • 2007 (NBA Draft) - Kevin Durant was considered by many to be one of the top two prosepects in the 2007 NBA Draft. The Seattle Super Sonics happily picked second. He was only picked behing Ohio State Center Greg Oden.
  • 2008 (Going to Oklahoma City) - Kevin only played one year with his Sonics. He moved with his team to Oklahoma City. He would now be part of the Thunder.
  • 2010 (NBA scoring leader) - Kevin Durant brought his "A" game every game in the 2010 season. He led the league in scoringwith 29.3 points per game.
  • 2010 (Leading Team USA) - Kevin Durant led team USA into the 2010 olympic games. He led the team in scoring and they won first place.
  • 2014 (Durant gets a ring!) - Kevin Durant leads his team to a victory in the ever so important game seven. He scored 35, his number, in the crutial game to beat the Celtics. This is the greatest accomplishment in the NBA for the greatest player in the NBA.
  • 2014 (MVP) - Durant was awarded the Most Valuable Player.
  • 2017-2018 (NBA champs) - Durant won back to back championships and got his 2nd and 3rd NBA ring.
